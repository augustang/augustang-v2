Make It Tangy

Parsons School of Design

Product design

Prototyping

User experience

A year long project, Parsons School of Design partnered with AREA 17 for a site redesign. As the designer, I collaborated closely with production, strategy, user experience, and engineering throughout the engagement. From ideation to high fidelity designs and prototypes to QA, my work spanned the design process and more. Systems thinking, rapid concepting/prototyping, and embracing the vast amount of content strategy were key in this project. Ultimately, the team produced over 500 new pages, dedicated over 3600 hours, and launched on time!
