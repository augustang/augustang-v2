Thousand Languages Project

Product design

Prototyping

User experience

Thousand Languages Project is an online database of literature translations. The identity captures the breadth and variety of written language across the globe and throughout history. Inspired by old star charts and the idea that constellations provide universal navigation, the identity includes a set of abstract, graphic shapes that are applied throughout the brand. These dynamic shapes, paired with imagery of writing, capture the texture of different languages and the spirit of the database.
