Campaigning

As the designer for Barrett, the Honors College, I created an interactive campaign to increase student enrollment in travel programs offered by the college. Students were able to view all postcard destinations, take a postcard, and find information about the program on the back. The campaign resulted in maximum enrollment in all travel programs offered that year.
