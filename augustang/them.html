Just for Them

Exhibit

Branding

Print

3D Design

“Just for Them,” was my senior thesis project in the Visual Communication Design program at Arizona State University. Through an educational lens, the goal was to raise awareness about the harm of the gender binary through brands and products perpetuating the binary. Visitors were encouraged to examine gendered products, and also read about the importance of pronouns, asking pronouns, and normalizing pronouns besides he/him and she/her. Participants received a free acrylic keychain etched with their pronouns.
