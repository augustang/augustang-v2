melissa m button

Melissa M Button is an artist and teacher whose work spans painting, mixed media, monotypes, and more. Her work is a window into her fascination with space and constructed realities. Drawing is foundational to her work. Melissa's variety of medium, fascinations, and drawing practice informed the direction, simplicity, and interactivity of the site.
