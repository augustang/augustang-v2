Emergence of Self

Exhibition

Art Direction

Book Arts

Acrylic Painting

Web Design

Emergence of Self explored gender identity and nonbinary existence through photography, book arts, painting, writing, and web design/development. Over the course of a year, I developed the many interdisciplinary facets to this project to facilitate conversation about nonbinary and trans identities, and through that, educate friends, family, and peers in my community. In November 2018, I had a solo exhibition showcasing my work to the public.
